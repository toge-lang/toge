## If/Else/Bif

Uppercase?whats that?no no sir its actually if/else/bif.
bif is actually short for "but if", being just a shorter elif.
anyways lets get to the point.
```
vrb("a", int, 3);
vrb("b", int, 2);
if(#a + #b ? 6) [wrt("Look!I KNEW 2 + 3 IS 6!!!")] --- no semicolon before bif or else, and neither after all conditions except for ---
bif(#a < #b) [wrt("LOOK! MAYBE 3 + 2 IS NOT 6 BUT 3 IS DEFINETELY SMALLER THAN 2!!! TRUST ME")]
else [wrt("math actually sucks")]
```
Straightforward enough.

## Until/For

Am i the only person who always hated "For" for some reason? It just makes no sense to me to be honest, while or until is just the same but more convenient.
Until is just a NOT version of while.

```
until(#a + #b ? 5) [wait(s, 2); wrt("LOOK! ITS REAL!!! THE 2 + 3 = 6 IS REAL!!!")]
for(#a; #a > 10; [wait(s, 1); #a = #a + 1]);
```
